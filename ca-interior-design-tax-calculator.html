CA Base Rate Structure (effective Jan 1, 2026): State 7.25% (incl. 1% Local Revenue Fund) + district/city voter-approved additions = your combined rate. The minimum in CA is 7.25% (some rural areas); most cities are 7.75%–10.75%; Lancaster and Palmdale are the highest at 11.25%.
